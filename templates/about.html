The Study:

About the Study (Improving Your Commute)

UC Berkeley in conjunction with the San Francisco MTA are looking to gather feedback and data regarding the transit experience of everyday communters just like you. Your privacy regarding your data is important to us, and you can view our confidentiality policies here.

What Does it Involve? (Using the App)

After installing the apk file onto your Android smartphone, you will be prompted to answer a few 10-15 second surveys following your trips on Muni. For the duration of the experiment, we will cover the costs of riding Muni.
