Using Muni


If you are new to using Muni, here are a few links to get you started:


  • Muni maps can be found at http://www.sfmta.com/maps. In particular, you’ll want to have a look at the system map: http://www.sfmta.com/maps/muni-system-map


  • To get schedule for your trip and find out what bus goes to destination, we recommend using Google Maps: http://maps.google.com. Click “Get Directions” on the left, then enter where you are now at the top and where you want to go at the bottom. You can also right-click on the map and select “Directions from here” and “Directions to here”. Then, on the bar with the vehicle symbols, click the transit symbol (the second from the left). This will open another dialogue asking you when you want to arrive or leave. Then hit “Get Directions”. Google will use the latest schedule information.


  • You can also find timetables at http://www.sfmta.com/getting-around/transit/schedules-trip-planners (this links to the 511 website).


  • To get real-time arrival predictions for Muni routes, visit http://www.nextmuni.com, then select your route, direction and stop.


  • If you are unfamiliar with the Clipper card, here’s a guide: http://www.clippercard.com/ClipperWeb/downloads/Clipper%20on%20Muni.pdf
